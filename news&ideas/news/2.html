Lumen Design receives lighting award

The Illuminating Engineering Society of North America (IES) recently announced
that Lumen Design and principal Andrew Pultorak, LC, MIES have been selected to
receive the esteemed Edwin F. Guth Memorial Award for Interior Lighting Design.
The award is in recognition of Lumen Design’s work on the lighting for Eye Society,
a boutique optical shop at the Bravern in Bellevue, WA.

The Edwin F. Guth Memorial Award is one of the IES Illumination Awards that
provide public recognition of professionalism, ingenuity, and originality in lighting
design.
