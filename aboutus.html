Andrew Pultorak

LC, MIES

Principal

Andrew has nearly 27 years of experience in the lighting industry. He worked as a Senior Lighting Designer for two Seattle firms before opening his own lighting design and consultation firm, Lumen Design, in 2008.

Andrew has designed interior and exterior lighting for private residences, restaurants, casinos, retail spaces, offices and houses of worship from Seattle to Spain to South Africa. He brings a sensitive, detail oriented approach and an effective, open communication style to all his projects. He’s created award winning work for Eye Society in Mill Creek, Washington, and the MGM Grand Gateway of Entertainment in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Andrew holds his certification from NCQLP (National Certification of Qualified Lighting Professionals), is a member of the IES (Illuminating Engineering Society), sits on the IES Puget Sound Section Board of Managers, served as President of the IES Puget Sound Section and is a member of the IES National Progress Report Committee.

Stephanie Lielke Wood

MIES, LEED AP, ASSOC. AIA

Senior Designer

Stephanie brings a complimentary set of skills, knowledge,
and experience to Lumen Design’s clients. Having spent an
accomplished 22 years in the lighting design industry, she is a
skilled CAD and hand drafter, with an eye for architectural
vision and an understanding of construction specifications.

Stephanie has managed hospitality, high end residential, and
commercial projects in the U.S. and abroad, ranging from
celebrity residences to world-class casinos in Las Vegas and beyond. Her educational background allows her to knowledgably collaborate with architects, engineers, and contractors while striving for consistent, high-quality results – on the job site and at the drawing board.

A graduate of the University of Washington, Stephanie holds
bachelor degrees in Architecture and Building Construction
Management. She is a member of IES (Illuminating
Engineering Society), the AIA (American Institute of
Architects), and is a LEED Accredited Professional.
